Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers pretend to be a trusted source to steal sensitive data like passwords, credit card details, or personal info. Common signs of phishing: Suspicious links or email attachments.
Messages creating urgency ("Your account will be locked!").
Unknown sender addresses. Consequences of Phishing: Financial Loss: Attackers can use stolen information to access bank accounts and make fraudulent transactions. Identity Theft: Personal information can be used to steal someone's identity. Malware Infection: Victims may inadvertently download malware, which can compromise their devices or systems.
